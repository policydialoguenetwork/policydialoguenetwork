Policy Dialogue Network

Policy Dialogue Network is a voluntary collective of researchers from different institutions aspiring to democratize and initiate policy dialogue. Envisaged through a network of researchers, collaborations with organizations, policy enthusiasts and grass-root level citizen participation, our attempt is to open up and diversify the policy space. The novelty of the effort lies in bringing fresh perspectives from young domain specialists to the crossroads of policy making. We propose to come up with policy reports and recommendations through in-depth research and inclusive participation.

Our Vision

PDN aspires to democratize the policy space by incorporating voices arising from the experience of citizens and the expertise of researchers. Committed to the ideals of inclusive and effective participation, we aim to initiate dialogue and diversify stakeholdership in policy making. Modelled on a bottom-up approach, PDN envisions ushering in policy engagement from the grassroots, thus strengthening a policy-informed society.

Our Mission
PDN's mission is to conduct and further participatory research on a broad spectrum of policy issues by engaging in independent research and encouraging collaborative research. By forging a network of domain specialists and engaging diverse stakeholders, PDN strives to put together policy reports and recommendations modelled on the idea of crowdsourcing.
