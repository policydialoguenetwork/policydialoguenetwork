About US

Policy Dialogue Network is a voluntary collective of researchers from different institutions aspiring to set up a think tank to democratise and initiate policy dialogue. Envisaged through a network of researchers, collaborations with organisations, policy enthusiasts and grass-root level citizen participation, our attempt is to open up and diversify the policy space. The novelty of the effort lies in bringing fresh perspectives from young domain specialists to the crossroads of policy making. We propose to come up with policy reports and recommendations through in-depth research and inclusive participation.

We are at the stage of expanding the idea and building the organisation. Our expectation is a commitment of 10-12 hours a week from our team members. Working with a broad spectrum of research interests we are looking for someone who is enthusiastic about diverse policy issues. Gathered under this umbrella, we see this as an opportunity to collaborate with like-minded specialists, be mentored by veterans and gain first-hand exposure to the policy space.
